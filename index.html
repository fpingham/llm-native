The LLM Native Manifesto

The Cognitive Automation Revolution

The Industrial Revolution birthed the factory, concentrating human labor in manufacturing centers. The Digital Revolution followed with the personal computer, creating digital workplaces that consumed our cognitive bandwidth. Just as factory automation liberated workers from mechanical tasks, cognitive automation is now freeing knowledge workers from routine computer operations.

People's potential shouldn't be consumed by routine digital operations - their unique capabilities shine in creative problem-solving, human relationships, and strategic thinking. Our mission is to liberate cognitive bandwidth from mechanical computer interactions, allowing focus on higher-value activities. To achieve this, we must minimize digital friction and manual computer operation. Technology should adapt to users, not force users to adapt to it.

The path forward is inevitable; cognitive automation is the natural step forward. LLMs are capability multipliers, enhancing rather than replacing human expertise. But implementation is everything. Our greatest challenge is experience design - taste is the ultimate currency. Technology must be smooth, clean, and invisible, enhancing work without disrupting it.

The LLM Native Company

Our objective is to create LLM native companies - organizations that fully embrace this new paradigm of cognitive automation. An LLM native company has LLMs engrained seamlessly in two fronts:

  • In the Team: LLMs become the cognitive middleware for every employee, turning intention into execution without digital friction.
  • In the Product: Intelligence is woven throughout the experience, handling complexity invisibly while flowing naturally with user actions.

Core Purpose

Our purpose is steadfast: to free human potential from routine digital operations, enabling professionals to focus on what really matters - creating quality products and services that serve our communities.
